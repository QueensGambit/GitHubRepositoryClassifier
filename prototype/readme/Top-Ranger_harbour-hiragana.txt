b'harbour-hiragana\n================\n\nSource code of https://openrepos.net/content/topranger/hiragana\n\nA simple Hiragana trainer for SailfishOS containing three test modes:\n\n  * Normal test: You get a Hiragana and have to choose the correct transcription\n  * Reverse test: You get a transcription and have to choose the correct Hiragana\n  * Free test: You get a  Hiragana and have to type in the correct transcription\n  * Free reverse test: You get a transcription and have to write the correct Hiragana\n    For now you have to decide yourself whether you answered a question correctly'
