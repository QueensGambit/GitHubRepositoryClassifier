The source\nis the XML version of the laws from\n[www.gesetze-im-internet.de](http://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/).\n\n\nWhy Git?\n----------\n\nAll German citizens can easily find an up-to-date version of their laws online.\nHowever, the legislation process, the historic evolution and the updates to laws\nare not easily and freely trackable. This allows the power of Git to be applied to the\nlegislation process. Integrating the whole history of German law changes in Git\nis the ambitious goal.\n\n\nWhy Markdown?\n-------------\n\nLaws are prose, they do not contain machine-readable semantics.\nA complex markup language like XML reduces the human-readability,\nmakes detection of differences harder and contains lots of\nunnecessary syntax.\n\nMarkdown is an intuitive formatting of text, that is readable and\nwritable by humans without the need of additional tools. That fits\nthe nature of laws that only need minimal formatting. Furthermore,\nMarkdown is machine-formattable and can be converted to other formats\nlike HTML.\n\n\nPull Requests\n-------------\n\nYou are encouraged to open pull request.
